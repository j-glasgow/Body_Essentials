Shipping Policy

  • Order processing takes 2-4 business days, although your order may ship sooner in some situations.
  • Monday through Friday, all orders are shipped via USPS (business days only excluding holidays). Depending on your location, delivery time after an order has shipped is generally 1-3 business days. Shipping confirmation and tracking information will be emailed to you.
  • All orders are sent from our headquarters in New York. The cost of shipping and handling is determined by the total weight of your item as well as your location. We charge a fixed price of $7 after 1 pound.
  • Please keep in mind that USPS tracking information can take up to 48 hours to update.
  • If your tracking information indicates that your package was delivered but you have yet to receive it, please contact the USPS at (800) 275-8777. Body Essentials is not liable for any packages that are delivered. Refunds are not given for packages that have been marked as delivered.
  • If your order is returned to us due to an erroneous address or an error on your part, you will be charged a re-shipping fee to reship your item.

    • Refunds & Exchange

  • Returns are presently not allowed due to the nature of our items. Nonetheless, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase; if you're not, please contact our customer service team, and we'll be happy to work with you to find a solution.
  • Unless there has been an error or fault on the part of Body Essentials, all sales are final.
  • Please contact us within two days of delivery if you suspect you received a defective item. In the event that Body Essentials discovers a flaw or damage in the product, we will replace it. Please take pictures of the problem, as well as the packing slip and order number.
  • On all orders and shipments, Body Essentials has the right to make the ultimate decision.
